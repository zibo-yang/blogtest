Anchor: Theorem thm:rho-hadamard  
Location: “set $x_j=\operatorname{sgn}(a_{ij})|a_{ij}|^{q-1}\bigl(\sum_j|a_{ij}|^q\bigr)^{(1-q)}$. Then $\|x\|_r^r=\sum_j|a_{ij}|^{(q-1)r}\bigl(\sum_j|a_{ij}|^q\bigr)^{(1-q)r}=1$ ... and $\sum_j a_{ij}x_j=\bigl(\sum_j|a_{ij}|^q\bigr)^{1/q}$.”  
Description: Critical Error: The normalization calculation is incorrect. If $S=\sum_j |a_{ij}|^q$, the displayed choice gives $\|x\|_r^r=S^{1-q}$, not $1$, and the resulting dot product is $S^{2-q}$, not generally $S^{1/q}$. Thus the proof of the row-norm lower bound is invalid, which breaks the lower-bound argument for $\rho_r^{(4)}(H)$.
