\]
Taking the supremum over nonzero \(x\) gives \(\le\|C\|_{\ell^r_k}\).”  
Description: Justification Gap: The displayed bound is only proved for the case \(u\ne0\), but the supremum is then taken over all nonzero \(x=(u,v)\), including vectors with \(u=0\). The omitted case is not explicitly handled.

Anchor: Theorem thm:rho-hadamard  
Location: “set \(x_j=\operatorname{sgn}(a_{ij})|a_{ij}|^{q-1}\bigl(\sum_j|a_{ij}|^q\bigr)^{(1-q)}\). Then \(\|x\|_r^r=\sum_j|a_{ij}|^{(q-1)r}\bigl(\sum_j|a_{ij}|^q\bigr)^{(1-q)r}=1\) … and \(\sum_j a_{ij}x_j=\bigl(\sum_j|a_{ij}|^q\bigr)^{1/q}\).”  
Description: Critical Error: The normalization calculation is false. If \(S=\sum_j |a_{ij}|^q\), the displayed expression gives \(\|x\|_r^r=S\cdot S^{-q}=S^{1-q}\), not \(1\) in general. Likewise the asserted value of \(\sum_j a_{ij}x_j\) does not follow. Thus the proof of the row-norm lower bound is invalid, and the subsequent lower bound for \(\rho_r^{(4)}(H)\) is not justified.
